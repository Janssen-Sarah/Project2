About me

Wondering why I went back to college? Or rather curious about the person behind this website? On this page you will find some more info about who I am and what I do in my spare time.

Find out more about me...

My name is Sarah Janssen. I am a creative with a passion for IT and technology. Ever since I was young, IT has been a big part of my life. Thanks to my studies in publicity and my training as a medical secretary, I already spent a lot of time behind my computer. Since my boyfriend graduated in 2021 as an IT graduate, it started itching more for me as well. It made me realize that it was time to chase my dream myself. Therefore, in early 2023, I decided to take a new path. After a great career as a medical secretary with an expert physician, I started a Programming course at Thomas More University of Applied Sciences. I tend to be very perfectionist, which means that I always strive for the highest possible quality and accuracy. This often leads to excellent results, but sometimes I spend too much time perfecting tasks. The benefit to my perfectionism is that I am always trying to deliver the best results. Besides constantly striving for the best quality, the urge to keep learning is something that motivates me to keep developing myself.

Hobbies and interests

Besides spending time behind my laptop, I also have other hobbies. For example, I love my little dog and spend a lot of time with him. I am also a passionate collector and caretaker of houseplants. In the past, I had a large collection of them. These days, because there are a lot of other challenges in my path, I have scaled back my plant collection a bit. Being a lover of nature, I love being outdoors. I enjoy all the beauty the environment has to offer. Together with my little dog, I often go out for long walks. We try to go on big trips every week. For me this is the ultimate way to escape from the daily hustle and bustle and unwind.
